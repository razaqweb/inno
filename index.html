WHAT WE DO

INNO is a non-profit organization specializing in youth development in Tanzania.

We provide outreach and workshop programmes to develop the necessary life skills a child would need to be valuable member of their community as they grow.

JOIN THE FAMILY

INNO is completely free to join, there are no tiers to membership either. However, please ensure you are committed to volunteering with us before considering signing up. Otherwise, follow the steps below to begin your registration:

  • Create a free INNO account. Don't worry, you won't be bombarded with marketing emails. This is just to confirm your identity for the next steps.
  • Fill out the registration form. Once you create an account, a registration form pdf will be sent to your email address. Print it, fill it out, sign it, then scan and email it back to us. Do not mail your registration form or deliver it yourself.
  • Attend the oral interview. The purpose of the interview is to verify some of your information and to get a better understanding of your competencies. You will receive your invitation for the interview if your registration is accepted.
